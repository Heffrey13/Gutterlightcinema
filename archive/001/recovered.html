Recovered Artifact

A heavily worn midnight admission ticket associated with Gutterlight Cinema. The printed feature title has been deliberately obscured, leaving no reliable record of the scheduled presentation.

A handwritten notation remains visible on the artifact. Archive personnel initially classified the marking as a personal reminder, though its later use as a valid recovery phrase suggests the ticket may have functioned as more than ordinary proof of admission.

The artifact appears to have survived prolonged storage under poor conditions. Surface wear, creasing, discoloration, and edge damage are consistent with repeated handling.
